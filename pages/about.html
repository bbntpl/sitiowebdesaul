About Me

Hi there! I'm Saul Goodman, but you can call me "Better Call Saul!" I'm a lawyer, and I specialize in helping my clients navigate the murky waters of criminal law.

My legal career has been a bit of a rollercoaster ride, but I've learned a lot along the way. I've represented all kinds of clients, from small-time crooks to big-time drug lords, and I've seen it all.

If you ever find yourself in legal trouble, don't hesitate to give me a call. I'll do everything in my power to help you out – and I'll look damn good doing it!
